About Me

Hi! I'm Yue Liu, a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Information Technology at Monash University, Australia, advised by Dr. Chakkrit Tantithamthavorn and Dr. Li Li. My research focuses on developing reliable and secure Large Language Models (LLMs) for software development, aiming to enhance developer productivity and software quality.

I received my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech) in 2019.
